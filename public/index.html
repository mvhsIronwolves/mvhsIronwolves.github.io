What is FTC?

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition for students in grades 7-12, designed to inspire interest in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).

How Does FTC Work?

In FTC, teams of up to 15 students design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. Each season revolves around a new game challenge, where teams must complete tasks or score points using their robots.

Building and Programming Robots

The robots are often built from a reusable platform, and teams use programming languages like Java to control them.

FTC Beyond Robotics

FTC encourages not only technical skills like engineering and programming but also teamwork, collaboration, and community outreach. Teams are judged on robot design, performance, innovation, and how they engage with their local and global communities through initiatives such as mentoring or teaching others.
